Protection program

In a journey spanning nearly a decade, the sponsorship and care of orphans has been at the core of ulfah Organization's work. As a result of this commitment, the number of orphans benefiting from sponsorship programs reached 30,203 by the year 2024.

Nutrition initiative

Our nutrition programs have provided over 5 million meals to children and families in food-insecure regions, helping combat malnutrition and promote healthy development.
03

Healthcare access

Through mobile clinics and partnerships with local hospitals, we've delivered essential medical care to over 1.2 million patients in underserved communities around the world.
04

Housing projects

Our shelter initiative has constructed over 300 durable homes for families displaced by conflict and natural disasters, providing safety and stability to vulnerable populations.
